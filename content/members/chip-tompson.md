Chip Tompson got his start drumming on the school cafeteria tables with such
conviction that a lunch lady once handed him an actual pair of sticks just
to make the noise more rhythmic. He auditioned for After School Special by
mailing in a cassette tape of himself keeping time to a washing machine on
spin cycle, and the band figured anyone with that kind of patience deserved
the gig.

Off stage, Chip is a two-time runner-up in a regional metronome-staring
contest and insists he can tune a drum kit using only vibes and a tuning
fork he "found." He's also the only member of the band legally banned from
one specific Guitar Center for "excessive enthusiasm."
