Lindsay Jurjovec claims to have been discovered singing to a parking lot full
of geese, who — she swears — applauded with their wings. Depending on who you
ask, she trained at either a prestigious European conservatory or "mostly
YouTube and vibes." She joined After School Special because, in her own
words, "someone finally brought snacks to rehearsal."

Off stage, Lindsay is a two-time, self-certified champion of competitive
minivan parallel parking, and once made an entire faculty meeting late
because she stopped to help a turtle cross Route 12.
