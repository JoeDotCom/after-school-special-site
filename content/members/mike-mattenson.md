Mike Mattenson was reportedly raised by a traveling circus of upright
bassists somewhere outside Waukegan, though he insists he "just really liked
the low notes" as a kid. Legend has it he once played a four-hour bass solo
at a Culver's grand opening and was asked, politely, to stop. He joined After
School Special after answering a Craigslist ad that simply read "Wanted:
someone who owns a bass amp," and never looked back.

When he's not holding down the groove, Mike can be found alphabetizing his
sock drawer by shade of white and arguing with strangers about the correct
way to load a dishwasher.
